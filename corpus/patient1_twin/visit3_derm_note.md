## Interval History

## Assessment
Chronic plaque psoriasis with significant objective progression on an adequate course of topical corticosteroid plus NB-UVB (39 sessions over 14 weeks). Disease is now moderate-to-severe by PASI/BSA/IGA with substantial quality-of-life impairment. Phototherapy plus topicals is no longer an adequate strategy as monotherapy. TB and hepatitis screening negative; no contraindication to biologic therapy.

## Plan
